Introducing Looopr: Walking Routes That Always Bring You Home

We built an app that generates circular walking loops with curated food, culture, and nature stops. Here's why.

We love walking. But we always had the same problem: where do I go? How far should I walk? And how do I get back without retracing my steps?

That frustration became the seed for Looopr — an app that generates circular walking routes based on one simple input: how long you want to walk.

The Problem with Walking Apps

Most navigation apps are built for getting from A to B. But walking isn't about the destination — it's about the journey. We wanted an app that understood that. One that would surprise you with a hidden courtyard, guide you past a great café, and always loop you back to where you started.

Existing walking apps either gave you straight-line routes (boring), relied on user-generated content (inconsistent), or required you to plan everything yourself (exhausting). We wanted something that felt effortless.

How Looopr Works

Open the app, set how long you want to walk — anywhere from 15 minutes to 3 hours — and tap "Find My Looopr." The app generates multiple circular routes near your location, each packed with curated points of interest:

  • Attractions — museums, parks, viewpoints, historic landmarks
  • Food & drink — cafés, restaurants, bakeries, all with ratings and opening hours
  • Hidden gems — the quiet garden, the street art alley, the bridge with the best view

Pick a route, tap start, and follow turn-by-turn navigation. Looopr alerts you when you're near a point of interest, lets you add food stops to your route on the fly, and tracks your walk with photos and stats.

Built for Discovery

Whether you're in your own neighbourhood or visiting a new city, Looopr finds walks you'd never find on your own. The algorithm considers walking pace, route shape, point-of-interest density, and even food quality ratings to generate routes that are genuinely worth taking.

The best walk is the one where you discover something you didn't know was there.

Every route is a loop. Every walk is a new discovery. And every step brings you closer to home.

What's Next

Looopr is available now on iOS. We're just getting started — upcoming features include walk sharing, community routes, premium multi-stop itineraries, and Android support.

Download Looopr for free and take your first loop today.
